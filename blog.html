Created by Emmanuel - 10th September 2022

Nigeria Christian Corpers' Fellowship came into existence as a result of a prophecy given some years ago by a notable British missionary; Pa S.G Elton. The prophecy goes thus “a time is coming when the government of this nation will be paying the youths of this nation to preach the gospel in all the nooks and crannies of the nation”.
